# Risk Disclosure

- **Past Performance Does Not Guarantee Future Returns:** All historical returns quoted (e.g., SGOV 1‑year 4.07%, US3MT yields, etc.) are provided for reference only and do not predict future performance.
- **Projected Returns Are Estimates, Not Promises:** The structured product coupon (5.94% for N02952, 3.93% for FXRA0415) is conditional on market conditions and may not be fully paid.
- **Structured Products Have Risk of Principal Loss:** If a callable note is called early or if the accrual condition is not met, investors may receive lower returns or, in a credit event, lose up to 100% of principal.
- **Currency Risk:** FXRA0415 is linked to USD/HKD. Changes in the peg mechanism could affect payouts.
- **Liquidity Risk:** Structured notes are not traded on exchanges; early redemption may incur losses.
