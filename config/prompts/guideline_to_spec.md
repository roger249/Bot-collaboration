# Guideline for a good specification

The workflow to producte the specification as follows.

- Identify the major workflow(s) of the required application
- From the workflow, identify the persona and artifact that could streamline the operation - consolidated report, filtered concise decision information, suggested actions from AI, etc.
- Some artefacts may be used in different workflow steps, or even different workflows.
- For each of the artifcact, put up a section specify its details
-- What is the purpose and benefit
-- Normally what user's action after using this.  e.g., calling client to make appointment, identify potential buyers with looming funding in next 3m.
-- The data in the artefact
-- The key filter to come up the data.
- Key assumptions in the specification
- Outstanding issue such as performance concern, or data availability to be stated

# Check list
- The workflow steps identified
- The workflow artifact identified
- Details of the workflow artifact are covered in seperate sections.
