## 📌 ETF Overview

### IWDA.L – iShares Core MSCI World UCITS ETF (Acc)
Tracks developed market large & mid-cap equities across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific.

### SPYL.L – S&P 500 UCITS ETF
Tracks the S&P 500 Index representing 500 largest US companies.  
*ETF share class may have <10Y history; 10Y figure reflects index-equivalent long-term performance.*

### 3191.HK – Global X China Semiconductor ETF
Targets Chinese semiconductor manufacturers and related technology firms.  
Launched 2020 – insufficient 5Y/10Y history.

### 2800.HK – Tracker Fund of Hong Kong
Tracks the Hang Seng Index representing large-cap Hong Kong-listed companies.

### N400.L – Invesco JPX-Nikkei 400 UCITS ETF
Tracks quality-focused Japanese large-cap companies selected based on profitability and governance metrics.

### EWU – iShares MSCI United Kingdom ETF
Tracks UK large & mid-cap equities across sectors.

### IHYA – iShares USD High Yield Corporate Bond UCITS ETF
Tracks USD-denominated global sub-investment-grade corporate bonds.
