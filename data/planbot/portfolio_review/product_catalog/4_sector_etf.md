# 📌 Sector Characteristics Overview

### XLK – Technology
High growth, AI, semiconductors, software. Highest long-term return profile among sectors.

### XLF – Financials
Banks, insurers, asset managers. Sensitive to interest rates & credit cycles.

### XLV – Health Care
Pharma, biotech, medical devices. Defensive with stable earnings.

### XLY – Consumer Discretionary
Retail, autos, e-commerce. Cyclical growth sector.

### XLP – Consumer Staples
Food, beverage, household goods. Defensive and dividend-oriented.

### XLE – Energy
Oil & gas majors and energy services. Commodity price driven.

### XLI – Industrials
Aerospace, machinery, transportation. Economic cycle exposure.

### XLB – Materials
Chemicals, mining, construction materials. Commodity-linked.

### XLRE – Real Estate
REIT exposure. Sensitive to interest rates.

### XLU – Utilities
Regulated utilities. Defensive, income-focused.

### XLC – Communication Services
Meta, Alphabet, telecom firms. Blend of growth & media exposure.

---

# Notes

- Technology and Energy have been the most volatile sectors over the past 5 years.
- Defensive sectors (Staples, Utilities, Health Care) typically show lower volatility but lower long-term growth.
