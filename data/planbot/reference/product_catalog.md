# Product catalog

This file contains the investment products available for the investor.

# Funds
- Money market fund denominated in USD, HKD or GBP
- Bond fund of various duration of corporate, sovereign, and high yield.
- Gold fund
- Crypto fund

# Stock universe
- Stocks included in the S&P 500, Hang Seng Index, or CSI-300
- Stock included listed in US and HKEX.

- Consumer Discretionary: Consumer Discretionary Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLY)
- Consumer Staples: Consumer Staples Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLP)
- Energy: Energy Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLE)
- Industrials: Industrial Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLI)

- Real Estate: Real Estate Select Sector SPDR Fund (XLRE)
